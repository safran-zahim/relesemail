# Email Generator User Manual

Welcome to the Email Generator! This tool allows you to create professional product release emails quickly and easily.

## Getting Started

When you first open the application, you'll see the **Release Email Editor**. This is where you'll spend most of your time customizing your email.

![Main Editor Interface](file:///C:/Users/Safran/.gemini/antigravity/brain/6842dcb5-2936-4a1c-8e2b-fee1968829d5/default_release_template_1777142911877.png)

## Core Sections

### 1. Branding & Identity
Set your company name, product name, version, and brand color. You can also upload your company logo.

### 2. Feature Categories
Organize your new features into categories. Each category can have its own icon and a list of improvements.
> [!TIP]
> We've simplified the default state to include just one sample category to help you get started quickly.

![Feature Categories](file:///C:/Users/Safran/.gemini/antigravity/brain/6842dcb5-2936-4a1c-8e2b-fee1968829d5/feature_categories_section_1777142940993.png)

### 3. New Feature Highlights
Highlight your most important features with a title, description, and an optional image or video link.

### 4. Hosted Environment
If you provide a demo system, you can include the login details here.

### 5. Stakeholder Demo
Add buttons that link to demo videos, slide decks, or release notes.

## Live Preview & Export

On the right side of the screen, you'll see a **Live Email Preview**. This shows exactly how your email will look in most email clients.

![Live Email Preview](file:///C:/Users/Safran/.gemini/antigravity/brain/6842dcb5-2936-4a1c-8e2b-fee1968829d5/live_email_preview_1777142948881.png)

### Exporting your Email
- **Copy HTML**: Copies the entire email's source code to your clipboard, ready to be pasted into your email sending tool.
- **Download**: Saves the email as an `.html` file on your computer.
- **Send Test**: Send a test email to yourself to verify the look and feel (requires backend configuration).

> [!IMPORTANT]
> Keep an eye on the **Payload Size** indicator. Most email clients (like Gmail) will clip emails that are larger than 102KB. Our tool tracks the size and warns you if you're approaching the limit.
